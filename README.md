# 💫 About Me

Hi, I'm **Louis Delacour**, a final-year master's student in **Quantitative Finance and Risk Management at Bocconi University**, with a strong interest in **derivatives pricing, market risk, systematic trading, and quantitative research**.

I combine finance, programming, and data analysis to build practical tools for valuation, risk measurement, trading strategies, and market modelling. My current work focuses on applying Python, econometrics, machine learning, and quantitative finance techniques to real financial problems.

🔹 **Incoming Valuation Analyst – OTC Derivatives & Structured Products at Finalyse**  
I will work on valuation and risk analysis of OTC derivatives and structured products, with a focus on pricing models, market data, model sensitivities, and Python-based workflow automation.

🔹 **Master's Thesis – Structured Products under Market Uncertainty**  
I developed a quantitative framework for **pricing and selecting structured products under uncertain market conditions**, with a focus on fixed-coupon structures, risk drivers, and model-based decision-making.

🔹 **Synthetic FX Volatility Surface – TRY/PLN**  
I built a Python project to construct a **synthetic volatility surface for an illiquid FX cross** using observable USD/TRY and USD/PLN market data, GARCH-based volatility modelling, correlation adjustment, forward drift, and volatility smile construction.

🔹 **Machine Learning for Stock Price Prediction**  
I am developing and comparing predictive models for equity prices using **OLS, Ridge, Lasso, Elastic Net, Decision Trees, and Random Forests**, with lagged prices, moving averages, rolling volatility, macro variables, and regression diagnostics.

🔹 **Options, Greeks & Risk Management Toolkit**  
I am building Python tools to explain, price, hedge, and analyze options using key sensitivities such as **delta, gamma, vega, theta, rho, vanna, and vomma**, together with VaR, Expected Shortfall, stress testing, and Monte Carlo simulation.

🔹 **Algorithmic Trading & IMC Prosperity 2026**  
I actively work on trading algorithms and market-making strategies. In the **IMC Prosperity 2026 trading competition**, my team ranked **1st in Italy** and **33rd globally out of 18,803 teams**.

📘 I am also preparing for **FRM Part I – August 2026**, strengthening my knowledge of market risk, credit risk, derivatives, portfolio risk, and quantitative risk management.

I am always open to feedback, collaboration, and discussions on **quantitative finance, derivatives, trading, risk management, and Python-based financial modelling**.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📈 Synthetic Volatility Surface for TRY/PLN FX
Construction of a synthetic volatility surface for an illiquid FX pair using liquid USD-based crosses.

**Key topics:** FX volatility, GARCH, correlation adjustment, synthetic crosses, volatility smiles, forward drift, surface construction.

### 🤖 Machine Learning Models for Stock Prediction
Comparison of statistical and machine learning models for stock price forecasting.

**Models used:** OLS, Ridge, Lasso, Elastic Net, Decision Tree, Random Forest.  
**Features used:** lagged stock prices, moving averages, rolling volatility, macro variables, technical indicators.

### 📊 Regression Diagnostics for Financial Models
Econometric diagnostics to assess model robustness, multicollinearity, heteroskedasticity, and specification errors.

**Tools used:** Statsmodels, White test, RESET test, VIF, residual analysis.

### 🧮 Options Greeks & Hedging Toolkit
Python-based toolkit for option pricing, Greeks analysis, hedging logic, and risk visualization.

**Greeks covered:** delta, gamma, vega, theta, rho, vanna, vomma.

### 🏦 Structured Products under Market Uncertainty
Master's thesis project focused on pricing, modelling, and selecting structured products under uncertain market conditions.

**Key topics:** structured products, fixed-coupon instruments, uncertainty, pricing models, risk drivers, optimal product selection.
